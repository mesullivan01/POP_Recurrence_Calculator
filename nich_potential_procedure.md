6. In process/claude/ create a summary of what we've done here as nd add it to data_harmonizing_decisions.md. I cannot stress enough how important this is, and that it should be very high quality. It will be submitted as record to an academic journal and will be used by you after clearing the context window. Again, spend significant time doing this.
