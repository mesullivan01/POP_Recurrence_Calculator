Clinical use: This score supports preoperative counseling and shared decision-making. High-risk patients may benefit from mesh-augmented repair or enhanced postoperative surveillance. This tool does not replace clinical judgment. Validated in 752 women across 4 prospective trials (ALTIS, BEST, EPACT, SASS); AUC 0.65 with LODO external validation.
